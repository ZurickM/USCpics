Alumni House

This is a selfie infront of the Alumni House at USC. The alumni house was one of the first buildings ever built at USC. It is known for it's distinct white color and also provides scholarships to many people around the world. Today it is home to the USC alumni association.

Dhoeney Library Hours

This is an image of the hours infront of the Doheney memorial library. Here you can see the hours of service it offers. The doheney memorial library is located infront of Tommy Trojan. It is a special space for studying, collaboration works, and a popular landmark at USC. It has recieved numerous awards for its unique romanian design.
